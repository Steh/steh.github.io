Rechte anzeigen

Get-Mailbox <mailbox>| Get-MailboxPermission | where User -like "S-*"

Rechte entfernen 

Get-Mailbox <mailbox>| Get-MailboxPermission | where User -like "S-*" | Remove-MailboxPermission
