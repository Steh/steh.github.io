should      = ratsam, advisable.
may         = mag, a possibility.  In questions :darf ich „ May I help you?“
must        = an obligation, a must
could       = ability, realistic
will        = a prediction or a promise for the future   
must not    = Verbot / taboo

shall = biblical outdated as in the Ten Commandments
        also heard in times gone by with „I“ and „we“ in questions. ( Shall we dance / Shall I make some tea?)
