## 🎯 Dial-In Pourover Recipe (Lance Hedrick Style, 3× Bloom)

### 📋 Brew Parameters V60

| Parameter               | 20 g Dose                       | 15 g Dose                     |
|-------------------------|--------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Grind Size              | Medium-coarse (~6.0–6.2)        | Medium-coarse (~6.0–6.2)       |
| Coffee Dose             | 20 g                           | 15 g                         |
| Ratio                   | 1:16 (320 ml water)            | ~1:16.7 (250 ml water)       |
| Bloom                   | 60 g water , 30–45 sec | 45 g water , 30–45 sec |
| 1st Pour                | +80 g (to 140 g total)         | +60 g (to 105 g total)        |
| 2nd Pour                | +100 g (to 240 g total)        | +70 g (to 175 g total)        |
| Final Top-Up            | +80 g (to 320 g total)         | +75 g (to 250 g total)        |
| Total Brew Time         | 2:30–3:00 min (from bloom start) | 2:15–2:45 min (from bloom start) |
| Cooling Method          | Ice bath or frother             | Ice bath or frother           |
| Serve                   | Over fresh ice                  | Over fresh ice                |

| Grinder             | Coffee Dose (g) | Grind Setting        | Filter Type            | Notes                                                      |
|---------------------|-----------------|----------------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 20              | 6.0–6.2              | V60 / Origami          | Balanced for clarity and flow                              |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 15              | 6.0–6.2              | V60 / Origami          | Use same setting as 20 g; adjust slightly if needed       |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 20              | 6.2–6.4              | Cafec Abaca            | Coarser for slower paper flow                              |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 15              | 6.2–6.4              | Cafec Abaca            | Same as 20 g dose, monitor flow and adjust                 |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 20              | 6.3–6.5              | CAFEC T-92 (Light Roast)| Coarser for light roasts; monitor brew time                |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 15              | 6.3–6.5              | CAFEC T-92 (Light Roast)| Same as 20 g dose; tweak based on taste                    |
| 1Zpresso J-Max      | 15–20           | ~30–32 clicks        | V60 / Origami          | Start medium-coarse; adjust finer if under-extracted      |
| Comandante C40      | 18–20           | ~25–28 clicks        | Hario V60              | Sweet and clean; adjust grind based on bean density       |

---

### ⚖️ Pouring Structure (With 3× Bloom Water)

| Step          | 20 g Dose                           | 15 g Dose                         |
|---------------|-----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Bloom         | Pour 60 g water, wait 30–45 sec   | Pour 45 g water, wait 30–45 sec  |
| 1st Pour      | Add 80 g (to 140 g total)          | Add 60 g (to 105 g total)         |
| 2nd Pour      | Add 100 g (to 240 g total)          | Add 70 g (to 175 g total)        |
| Final Top-Up  | Add 80 g (to 320 g total)          | Add 75 g (to 250 g total)        |

---

### ⚖️ Brew Structure by Dose Size (1:16 & ~1:16.7 Ratios)

| Coffee Dose | Total Water           | Bloom Water | 1st Pour         | 2nd Pour         | Final Top-Up      | Total Water |
|-------------|-----------------------|-------------|------------------|------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| 20 g        | 320 g (1:16)          | 60 g        | 80 g → 140 g     | 100 g → 240 g    | 80 g              | 320 g       |
| 15 g        | 250 g (~1:16.7)       | 45 g        | 60 g → 105 g     | 70 g → 175 g     | 75 g              | 250 g       |

---

### 🧠 Tips

- Adjust grind coarseness slightly coarser for slower filters like CAFEC T-92 and Cafec Abaca to maintain proper flow.
- Bloom water at ~3× coffee weight improves extraction uniformity.
- Maintain total brew time between 2:15–3:00 minutes depending on dose.
- Use gentle pouring to avoid channeling.

## 🎯 Two-Pour Dial-In Pourover Recipe (Fellow Ode Gen 2 – Lance Hedrick Style, 3× Bloom)

### 📋 Brew Parameters

| Parameter               | 20 g Dose                       | 15 g Dose                     |
|-------------------------|--------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Coffee Dose             | 20 g                           | 15 g                         |
| Ratio                   | 1:16 (330 ml water)            | 1:16 (250 ml water)       |
| Bloom                   | 60 g water (3× coffee), 30–45 sec | 45 g water (3× coffee), 30–45 sec |
| Rest Pour               | 270 g (to 330 g total)         | 205 g (to 250 g total)        |
| Total Brew Time         | 2:30–3:00 min (from bloom start) | 2:15–2:45 min (from bloom start) |

---

### ⚙️ Grinder, Coffee Dose & Filter Notes

| Grinder             | Coffee Dose (g) | Grind Setting        | Filter Type            | Notes                                                      |
|---------------------|-----------------|----------------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 20              | 6.1              | hario V60          | Balanced for clarity and flow                              |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 15              | 6.0              |  hario V60          | Use same setting as 20 g; adjust slightly if needed       |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 20              | 6.2              | CAFEC T-92 (Light Roast)| Coarser for light roasts; monitor brew time                |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2    | 15              | 6.1              | CAFEC T-92 (Light Roast)| Same as 20 g dose; tweak based on taste                    |

---

### ⚖️ Two-Pour Brew Structure (3× Bloom)

| Step        | 20 g Dose                        | 15 g Dose                        |
|-------------|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Bloom       | 60 g water, 30–45 sec            | 45 g water, 30–45 sec            |
| Rest Pour   | 260 g water, slow/even pour      | 205 g water, slow/even pour      |
| Chill/Serve | Ice bath/frother, serve over ice | Ice bath/frother, serve over ice |

---

### 🧠 Tips

- Bloom with 3× coffee weight for even extraction.
- Pour rest gently to avoid channeling.
- Adjust grind if brew time is outside target range.
- Flash chill for best flavor.

## 🧊 Flash Brew Iced Coffee (Lance Hedrick Style)

### Brew Parameters

| Parameter          | 20 g (Default)           | 15 g (Small Batch)        |
|--------------------|-------------------------|---------------------------|
| Grinder            | Fellow Ode Gen 2        | Fellow Ode Gen 2          |
| Grind Size         | Medium-coarse (≈6.1)    | Medium-coarse (≈6.0)      |
| Coffee             | 20 g                    | 15 g                      |
| Total Water        | 300 g                   | 225 g                     |
| Bloom              | 60 g (30–45 sec)        | 45 g (30–45 sec)          |
| 1st Pour           | +90 g (to 130 g, 1:00)  | +67.5 g (to 112.5 g)      |
| 2nd Pour           | +90 g (to 220 g, 1:45)  | +67.5 g (to 180 g)        |
| Final Top-Up (ice) | To 300 g                | To 225 g                  |

---

### Pouring Steps

- **0:00** Bloom (60 g or 45 g). Wait 30–45 sec.
- **1:00** Pour 90 g (or 67.5 g).
- **1:45** Pour 90 g (or 67.5 g) again, swirl gently. Wait until drained.
- **2:30** Add ice to reach final weight (60 g or 45 g). Stir to cool (5–10 °C).
- **3:00** Serve over fresh ice cubes.

---

### Grinder & Brew Notes

| Grinder           | Dose / Setting | Filter Type              | Notes                           |
|-------------------|---------------|--------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Fellow Ode Gen 2  | 15 g / 6.0    | Hario V60                | Balanced flow and clarity       |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2  | 20 g / 6.1    | Hario V60                |                                 |
| Fellow Ode Gen 2  | 15 g / 6.1    | CAFEC T-92 (light roast) | For slow filters, grind coarser |

---

## Pour Notes

- Bloom: **30–45 sec**
- Pause: Let water nearly drain (~30 sec) between main pours
- Ice: Add cubes one by one, wait for each to melt before adding the next

---

## Tips

- Rinse filters thoroughly to avoid papery taste
- For slow filters (e.g., CAFEC), grind coarser or shorten contact time
- Adjust grind if brew time is not 2:30–3:00 min
