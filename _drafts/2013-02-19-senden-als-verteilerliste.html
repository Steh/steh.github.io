Senden-Als vergeben

[code lang="powershell" light="true"]Add-ADPermission <em>AD-Objekt</em> -ExtendedRights Send-As -User <em>Benutzer</em> -AccessRights ExtendedRight | fl[/code]

Senden-Als anzeigen

[code lang="powershell" light="true"]Get-ADPermission <em>AD-Object</em> | Where-Object {$_.ExtendedRights -like "*Send-as*"}[/code]
