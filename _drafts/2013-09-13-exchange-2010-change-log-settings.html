--- layout: post title: 'Exchange 2010: Change Log Settings' categories: - Exchange tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _wpas_done_all: '1' sharing_disabled: '1' _edit_last: '1' --- The Log Settings are located in ..\MicrosoftExchange Server\V14\Bin.
  • microsoft.exchange.addressbook.service.exe.config
  • Microsoft.Exchange.RpcClientAccess.Service.exe.config
  • ...
In these Files you can Change the:
  • LogPath
  • MaxDirectorySize
  • MaxRetentionPeriod
After Changes you have to restart the affected Service.
