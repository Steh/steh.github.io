Preparation

1. Download Exchange 2010 SP3 2. Extract the Service Pack on your Exchange Server 
If you are in an Multi-Domain-Environment, you shout make the Schema Update from your Schema Master.
How to find your Schema Master: [code lang="powershell"]Get-ADForest | Select-Object SchemaMaster[/code]

Schema Master

1. Copy Exchange Setup to the Schema Master 2. Open CMD or PowerShell with Administrator Rights 2.1. setup.com /PrepareSchema 2.2. setup.com /PrepareAD 2.3. setup.com /PrepareAllDomains 3. Wait for the Replication in your Environment
